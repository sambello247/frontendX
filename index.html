FrontendX Boilerplate (HTML, CSS, SCSS, JS)

Welcome to FrontendX Boilerplate, your go-to resource for kickstarting your web development projects with ease and efficiency. Our mission is to empower web developers of all skill levels by providing a robust and customizable foundation for building modern, responsive, and beautiful websites and web applications.


At FrontendX, we understand the challenges developers face when starting a new project. The process can be overwhelming, time-consuming, and filled with repetitive tasks. That's why we've created this comprehensive frontend boilerplate, designed to streamline your development workflow and save you valuable time.
